Short guide & 30-second marketing video for team use

Why I Chose the University of Minnesota Duluth

I chose the University of Minnesota Duluth because I’m playing collegiate hockey here, and I absolutely love the program. The rink is incredible—one of the best facilities I’ve ever played in—and being part of the team has been an amazing experience.

What I Like About Duluth

I love Duluth, especially in the fall when all the leaves change color. The scenery around Lake Superior is beautiful, and there are so many great local restaurants to explore. The lake is one of my favorite spots—it’s peaceful and a great place to relax or hang out with friends.

Personal Experiences and Campus Highlights

Being a student-athlete at UMD has been a dream come true. Outside of hockey, I’ve made a lot of great memories—like going swimming in the lake with my teammates and grabbing ice cream at Love Creamery. The community here is supportive, the campus is beautiful, and there’s always something to do.
