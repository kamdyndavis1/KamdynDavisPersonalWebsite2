Why I Chose the University of Minnesota Duluth

I chose the University of Minnesota Duluth because I’m playing collegiate hockey here, and I absolutely love the program. The rink is incredible—one of the best facilities I’ve ever played in—and being part of the team has been an amazing experience.

What I Like About Duluth

I love Duluth, especially in the fall when all the leaves change color. The scenery around Lake Superior is beautiful, and there are so many great local restaurants to explore. The lake is one of my favorite spots—it’s peaceful and a great place to relax or hang out with friends.

Personal Experiences and Campus Highlights

Being a student-athlete at UMD has been a dream come true. Outside of hockey, I’ve made a lot of great memories—like going swimming in the lake with my teammates and grabbing ice cream at Love Creamery. The community here is supportive, the campus is beautiful, and there’s always something to do.

Hobby — Hockey

Hockey has always been more than just a sport for me—it’s my passion and favorite hobby. Playing hockey allows me to challenge myself physically and mentally while being part of a close-knit team. The fast pace of the game teaches me focus, quick decision-making, and perseverance. I love the feeling of skating on the ice, the teamwork it takes to set up a good play, and the sense of accomplishment after a hard-fought game. Beyond competition, hockey helps me build leadership skills, discipline, and strong friendships that extend off the rink. It’s also a great outlet for stress and keeps me active and healthy. Whether I’m practicing, competing, or coaching younger players, hockey continues to shape who I am and motivates me to work hard in every area of life.

Hobby — Country Concerts

I love going to country concerts — the energy of the crowd, the storytelling in the music, and the sense of community make live shows unforgettable. Whether it's a large outdoor festival or a smaller venue, I enjoy singing along to favorite songs, discovering new artists, and sharing the experience with friends. Concerts are a great way to unwind and celebrate with people who appreciate the same music.
